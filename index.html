Improv Model Questionnaire

Goal of the questionnaire

This questionnaire is developed as a means of getting insight into your own improvisation. It is therefore important that you focus on the actual drama that you have displayed during the improvisation exercises when answering these questions.

Guidelines

This questionnaire consists of sixteen propositions regarding your improvisation play. Per proposition you have five points to divide over the statements (a) through (d). The answer that applies best to your play gets the highest rating (maximum 5). The answer that least applies gets the lowest (minimum 0). Per question you must assign exactly five points in total across a, b, c and d.

Example: If you put

2 on (a), 1 on (b), 2 on (c), and 0 on (d),

that’s 5 points total.
